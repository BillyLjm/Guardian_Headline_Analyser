Hello!

This is a data analyser for Guardian news articles.
This simply searches the Guardian database for articles relevant to your search query.
Afterwhich, this website will parse and organise all the data returned,
Outputting the no. of articles published monthly, common words used in each headlines & the sections categorisation.
(Note: The most common words are parsed from headlines only, the words in the content of the articles are not represented)

How to Use

Simply click on the 'Search' button at the top of the page to access the search form.

Search Words to search for, just like a Google search.
Date Time period to return articles from. The default values are from "the beginning of time" to "now".
Sort By Choose to return the newest or oldest articles first.
This field will only have an effect when used in conjunction with the 'Maximum Number' field
Section Which section of Guardian news to look in. If none are selected, all sections will be searched for.
Max No. Maximum number of (relevant) articles to analyse. If empty, all relevant articles returned by Guardian will be analysed.
However, the more articles analysed, the longer the webpage will take to analyse & return the results.
